Avery's Riddle Me This?

Welcome to Avery's Riddle Me This? game! Choose your level and solve the riddles.

Game Rules

  • Rule 1: You must solve the riddle to advance to the next level.
  • Rule 2: You can only submit one answer at a time.
  • Rule 3: If you get stuck, you can ask for a hint.

Levels

  • Level 1: Easy
  • Level 2: Medium
  • Level 3: Hard

How to Play

Choose a level and try to solve the riddle presented. You can submit your answer, and if it's correct, you'll move on to the next riddle. If you get stuck, you can ask for a hint.

You can try to stump me with a riddle of your own!

Hints

  • Hint 1: Think about the keywords in the riddle.
  • Hint 2: Break the riddle down into smaller parts.
  • Hint 3: Don't be afraid to ask for help!
